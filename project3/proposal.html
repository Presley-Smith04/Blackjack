Blackjack

I. High Concept

A simple blackjack game.

Use one of these techniques:

  • Primary Action - While counting your own cards, keep in mind the potential danger that the dealer could have

II. Genre

Casino Card Game

III. Platform

Universal

IV. Story

Simple blackjack game, where the goal is to get your card total as close to 21 as possible.

It is a game playing against the dealer trying to decide if you have enough value in your hand

to beat the dealer without going over 21. The only indicator is one dealer card is shown, the other... hidden

V. Esthetics

Graphics style - semi-realistic

Sound - simple casino music

VI. Gameplay

Mechanics

What specific choices can the player make and what can they do?

Here are some compilations of game mechanics you might find helpful:

  • The player can hit (add a card to their hand)
  • The player can stand (stop adding cards to their hand, revealing the dealers hand)
  • The player can reset using a button

Control

  • Keyboard and/or mouse
  • Touch and/or gesture (Mobile only)

Teaching the game/New user experience aka "Onboarding"

There is a seperate page teaching the player the basic rules and a basic strategy when playing blackjack

Player learning

The player should be able to count, and the player should be able to make an educated guess on what totals should be hittable vs standable
